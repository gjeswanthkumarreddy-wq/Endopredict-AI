About EndoPredict AI

EndoPredict AI is an intelligent healthcare decision-support system designed to assist in the early prediction of Endometriosis using Machine Learning techniques. The system analyzes patient information such as age, chronic pain level, menstrual irregularity, hormone level abnormalities, infertility history, and BMI to estimate the likelihood of Endometriosis.

Users enter patient details through the prediction interface. The trained AI model processes the data and generates a prediction result along with confidence metrics. This helps healthcare professionals make faster and more informed clinical decisions.
